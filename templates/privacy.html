Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

We collect the information you provide when you create an account (name, email address, password) and the expense data you enter while using Spendly (amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions). We do not collect payment information.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service — displaying your expenses, generating summaries, and personalising your experience. We do not use your financial data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly uses Google Fonts to load typefaces. No personal or financial data is shared with any third-party service. We do not integrate analytics platforms, advertising networks, or data brokers.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us at privacy@spendly.app. We aim to respond within 5 business days.
